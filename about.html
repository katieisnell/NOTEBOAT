What is NoteBoat?

NoteBoat is a free-to-use revision planner and academic sharing platform, designed for Computer Science and Mathematics students at the University of Manchester. Here users can create personalised revision timetables and share lecture and revision notes with others.

Who are we?

Our team is made up of first year Computer Science and Maths students and managed by our tutor Renate Schmidt. The team members are Matt Cutler, Poppy Reid, Suvineet Singh, Katie Snell, Abdur Suleman, Jessica Yu and Eliot O'Brien.

Creating NoteBoat

NoteBoat was created as part of our first year at the University of Manchester. Over a period of several months we designed and implemeted this web application while learning web programming techniques along the way.
